## Concept 1: Tech Circuit Grid + Building Structure
**File**: `public/logo-v2-concept-1.svg`  
**Tagline**: "TECHNOLOGY + INFRASTRUCTURE"

### Design Philosophy
**Visual Concept**: Building grid (infrastructure) merged with tech circuit connections (technology)
- Background: 3×3 grid of squares (representing built/engineered structures)
- Overlay: Gold circuit lines connecting through the grid (showing technology flowing through infrastructure)
- Connection nodes: Gold dots showing data/tech integration points

### What It Represents
- **Left to right**: Infrastructure → Technology flow
- **The grid**: Your construction, manufacturing, and real estate capability
- **The circuits**: Tech solutions enabling infrastructure projects
- **Integration point**: Your ability to blend physical + digital solutions

### Best For
- B2B clients (EPC, manufacturing, real estate companies)
- Emphasizes capability across multiple sectors
- Shows you're not just consultants, but builders/doers

### Trademark Distinctiveness
✅ Unique combination of grid + circuit overlay  
✅ Specific gold node placement  
✅ Original visual metaphor

## Concept 3: Interconnected Ecosystem Hub
**File**: `public/logo-v2-concept-3.svg`  
**Tagline**: "INTEGRATED MULTI-SECTOR SOLUTIONS"

### Design Philosophy
**Visual Concept**: Six sectors/services connecting through central integration hub
- Left column: Three nodes (tech/digital services)
- Center hub: Gold core (your integration point)
- Right column: Three nodes (infrastructure/physical)
- Connections: Gold lines showing all sectors interconnected

### What It Represents
- **Two columns**: Tech side (left) ↔ Infrastructure side (right)
- **Center hub**: Where all integration happens
- **Connections**: Your ability to unify 15+ different sectors
- **Ecosystem**: Holistic, integrated approach to business solutions

### Best For
- Positioning as integrated multi-sector player
- Attracting partners across different industries
- Communicating "we understand ecosystem integration"
- Companies seeking comprehensive, multi-service providers

### Trademark Distinctiveness
✅ Unique 6-node + hub network pattern  
✅ Original interconnection visualization  
✅ Distinctive ecosystem representation
