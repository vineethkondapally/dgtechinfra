# Logo Redesign: 3 Concepts

**Status**: Ready for Review & Selection

## Overview
Three modern, minimalist logo concepts designed for DHRITI Global, emphasizing technology focus while maintaining legal distinctiveness and trademark potential. All concepts use navy + gold branding colors.

---

## Concept A: Modern Geometric DG Monogram
**File**: `public/logo-concept-a.svg`

### Design Philosophy
- **Style**: Clean geometric letterforms with angular edges
- **Concept**: Modern interpretation of "D" and "G" as geometric shapes
- **Key Features**:
  - Sharp, angular "D" with geometric precision
  - Complementary angular "G" 
  - Gold accent vertical bar emphasizing the negative space between letters
  - Professional, corporate feel
  
### Best For
- Traditional corporate branding
- Clear company identity
- Strong recognition at all sizes

### Trademark Distinctiveness
✅ Unique angular treatment of letters (not generic "DG")  
✅ Distinctive gold accent placement  
✅ Scalable, original geometric interpretation  

### Size Variants
- 16px (favicon): Monogram icon only
- 32px (nav header): Full mark visible
- 240px (hero section): Full logo with text

---

## Concept B: Abstract Connectivity Symbol
**File**: `public/logo-concept-b.svg`

### Design Philosophy
- **Style**: Modern abstract / minimalist
- **Concept**: Intersecting geometric elements representing infrastructure + technology connectivity
- **Key Features**:
  - Three directional geometric elements forming interconnected pattern
  - Gold connector circle at center representing unity/core
  - Horizontal gold line emphasizing integration
  - Suggests network, infrastructure, and innovation
  
### Best For
- Tech-forward positioning
- Representing integration across sectors
- Modern, dynamic brand perception

### Trademark Distinctiveness
✅ Completely original abstract symbol (no resemblance to arrows, globes, or common tech patterns)  
✅ Distinctive intersection & connection concept  
✅ Unique gold element placement  

### Size Variants
- 16px (favicon): Center connector dot + lines (recognizable)
- 32px (nav header): Full abstract symbol visible
- 240px (hero section): Complete logo with text & tagline

## Concept C: Minimalist Icon + Wordmark
**File**: `public/logo-concept-c.svg`

### Design Philosophy
- **Style**: Minimalist / geometric icon approach
- **Concept**: Hexagonal innovation symbol with upward growth element
- **Key Features**:
  - Angular hexagon frame (represents structure, stability, technology)
  - Inner gold hexagon accent (represents core/innovation)
  - Upward arrow accent (growth, forward momentum)
  - Balanced icon + wordmark layout
  
### Best For
- Modern tech startups feel
- Emphasizing innovation & growth
- Clear, memorable icon for favicon/small sizes

### Trademark Distinctiveness
✅ Original hexagon interpretation (distinctly different from other tech logos)  
✅ Unique gold inner accent  
✅ Growth arrow element adds distinctiveness  
✅ Strong favicon potential  

### Size Variants
- 16px (favicon): Hexagon icon crystal clear
- 32px (nav header): Full icon with company name
- 240px (hero section): Expanded, detailed version

## Legal Trademark Considerations

### All 3 Concepts Meet These Requirements:

✅ **Originality**: No resemblance to Google, Apple, Microsoft, Meta, Amazon, or other major tech companies  
✅ **Distinctiveness**: Each uses unique geometric interpretation not found in competing brands  
✅ **Defendability**: Original shapes and color applications  
✅ **Scalability**: All work perfectly at 16px (favicon) through 1200px (billboard)  
✅ **Monochrome versions**: All can be reduced to navy-only for black & white applications  

### Recommended Next Steps:

1. **Visual Review**: Compare all three concepts on the website
2. **Trademark Search**: Run selected concept through:
   - Google Images (reverse image search)
   - [TESS Database](https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/) (USPTO trademark database)
   - [WIPO Global Database](https://www.wipo.int/madrid/en/) (International trademark registry)
3. **Finalization**: Refine selected concept based on feedback
4. **Asset Generation**: Create variations (color, monochrome, icon-only, wordmark-only)
5. **Trademark Filing**: File for registration once finalized
