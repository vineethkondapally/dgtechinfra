📊 Concept Comparison & Recommendations

✓ Refinement & Evolution

  • Best for: Representing growth and technology
  • Pros: Dynamic, modern, shows upward momentum
  • Cons: Complex, may not scale well at small sizes

✓ Minimalist Interlink

  • Best for: Digital platforms, tech-forward brands
  • Pros: Clean, modern, scalable
  • Cons: Minimal visual interest, may feel cold

✓ Tech-Infra OT

  • Best for: Operations & Technology messaging
  • Pros: Memorable, meaningful letters, professional
  • Cons: Specific to O&T acronym

✓ Tech-Infra Bridge

  • Best for: Emphasizing connectivity and spanning gaps
  • Pros: Iconic, instantly recognizable concept
  • Cons: Vertical orientation, slightly complex

✓ Global Network Weave

  • Best for: Global network and interconnection
  • Pros: Sophisticated, represents complexity
  • Cons: May feel busy in small sizes
